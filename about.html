About

Less setup-and-punchline, more "let me tell you what happened."

Relying less on jokes and more on a storytelling style, Steve deals with topics ranging from his own health (poor) to the political climate (even worse).

His background in improv and theater brings a level of comfort to the stage that always translates into a great show — the kind of set that feels less like a routine and more like a conversation that got a little out of hand.

"His loud, opinionated and charming personality is made for the stage." Creative Loafing Tampa

Tampa's Creative Loafing magazine named Steve Best Young Comic for 2012, and readers voted him Best Local Stand-Up Comedian in both 2014 and 2018. He's shared the stage with some of comedy's biggest names, been heard on Sirius/XM satellite radio, and seen on Fox television.
